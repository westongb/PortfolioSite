About

I have always loved working with techonology. As a young kid I loved finding new programs and spending hours exploring its capabilities. I remember the day I disovered PowerPoint. That night I went home and spent hours building the equivilant of a "choose your own adventure story". This desire to explore possibilites helped me develop a talent in breaking down processes, and finding solutions. I got a degree in Economics and went to work in buisness administration and oporations. But I didn't like being a gofer. I wanted to help design and innovate, so I focused on learning communication and leadership skills. With these new skills I worked in sales and managed a call center team of nine collectors. But my interest in technology continued, and I ended up working on multiple development projects at these companies. At this time I decided to go work for Helio Training, and worked side by side with experienced developers every day. My interest in coding grew and now I had constant access to individuals and recourses that could help me learn. So, I decided to learn for my self. With the help of developers I new knew in the industry, I spent 8 months teaching my self full stack web development focusing on the MERN stack. Now I have developed multiple web pages and applications and am excited to start my new career as full time developer.
