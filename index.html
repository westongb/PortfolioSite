About Me

I have always loved working with techonology. As a young kid I loved finding new programs and spending hours exploring its capabilities. I remember the day I disovered PowerPoint. That night I went home and spent hours building the equivilant of a "choose your own adventure story". This desire to explore possibilites helped me develop a talent in breaking down processes, and finding solutions. I got a degree in economics and went to work in business administration and operations. But I didn't like being a gofer. I wanted to help design and innovate, so I focused on learning communication and leadership skills.

With these new skills I worked in sales and managed a call center team of nine collectors. But my interest in technology continued, and I ended up working on multiple development projects at these companies. At this time I decided to go work for Helio Training, and worked side by side with experienced developers every day. My interest in coding grew and now I had constant access to individuals and recourses that could help me learn. So, I decided to learn for my self. With the help of developers I new knew in the industry, I spent 8 months teaching my self full stack web development focusing on the MERN stack. Now I have developed multiple web pages and applications and am excited to start my new career as full time developer.

I HATE Peas.

As I have worked to learn full stack development I have had the opportunity to work with multiple technologies and frameworks. I realize that each has its own strengths and drawbacks.

I love the event and state handling of ReactJS, and yet I am drawn to the clean organization of VueJS. MongoDB allows for a great degree of freedom while the structure of Postgres keeps your data clean and organized.

This has driven home to me the importance of continually learning as a developer. There are so many different ways to approach building software, and it is the job of the developer to know the best tool for each senario. Or at least which tool he "thinks" is best for the situaiton. ðŸ˜‰
