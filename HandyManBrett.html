Every company needs a website, even if your job has nothing to do with technology. So when Brett White wanted to start his Handy Man buisness he needed a website to allow him to gather leads, book jobs and advertise his services.

Vue and Node

I wanted to use this chance to expore new technolgies and specifically to use VueJS together with Nodejs. The Vue format keeps your HTML, CSS and Javascript in a clean format helping keep everything organized.

Expess with JSON

Clients need to know what jobs Brett can do, and what he can't. So a list of service catagories is important, but it is not confidential. So rather than integrate a database, I simply built the catagories and descriptions into a JSON file and can be queried with Express.

Meet Brett

Knowing who you are working with is everything in a service industry. So it was very important the a lot of the site content be dedicated to helping visitors get to know Brett. Who he is and what he can do for them.

EmailJs

Brett is not the most Tech savy individual and does't want a CRM. So instead I integrated EmailJs to send Brett an email with the customers information after they fill out the form. Tihs allows him to store the information any way he wishes.

Heroku Web Hosting

Since this is a small buisness with minimal traffic we decided there was no need to pay for hosing at them moment when Heroku offers free hosting. I hosted the Front end and Back ends of the application on heroku, which cost the client nothing.
